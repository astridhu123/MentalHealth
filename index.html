Smartphones & Emotional Wellbeing

Exploring the hidden connection between our devices and our emotions.

What's the Truth about Social Media?

Using social media sites such as Instagram has become part of the daily routine for many people.

We habitually check our news feeds for updates from our friends, as well as news media, celebrities, or brands.

Optimists have often argued that social media sites use helps to strengthen social relationships.

Meanwhile, pessimists argue that social media and mobile devices only give people the illusion of connection.

Did You Know?

Studies integrating social media use, social support, and health outcomes are rare.

Research has mainly focused on two separate areas:

  • The relationship between social support and health

  • The link between social media use and social support

We aim to combine these areas to explore whether online social support from social media is beneficial.
